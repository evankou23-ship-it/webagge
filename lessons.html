Lessons Learned

I learned all of these things from real experiences. Think of them as canon events that needed to happen or else I'd never truly understand the value.

  1. I offered to help an older man in the Oswego Walmart find some things, then he offered to pay for my cart full of items, but I respectfully insisted on declining.

    2. Lesson: Accept people's kindness. It could save you $50 too.

  2. I cared about Mat Sci so little, they gave me my worst grade for a class to date in it.

    3. Lesson: Even though something is easy, make sure you do it right or else it'll be embarassing to yourself when you do it poorly.

  3. Someone swiped my mirror while I was driving on Jericho, I was too stunned to even do anything -- just look at the criminal. They sped away afterward.

    4. Lesson: If someone wrongs you on the road, just snap their license plate...saves $500...
